# Admit a registered attendee

As a delivery coordinator, I want to admit a participant whose registration is
"confirmed" so that attendance may be recorded for the workshop.

Registration owns seat commitment and release. Delivery owns admission and
attendance evidence. The available business evidence does not establish which
Registration-owned fact, if any, is sufficient for Delivery to admit someone;
nor does it define how Delivery names or translates that fact. No relationship
or cross-context contract has been accepted.
